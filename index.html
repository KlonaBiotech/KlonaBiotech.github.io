ABOUT THE COMPANY


At Klona Biotech, we aim to pioneer new methods for rapid and scalable production of biological structures which combinine the precision of 3D printing with the integrity of woven and fibrous scaffolds.


Our goal is to provide tools for tissue engineering, regenerative medicine, and medical implantation that surpass conventional limitations in both strength and biocompatibility.





OUR LONG-TERM VISION


We envision a future where structurally-sound, patient-specific biological implants can be fabricated rapidly and affordably. Our technology is designed to be modular and scalable. Klona aims to partner with research hospitals to accelerate pre-clinical testing and regulatory validation.
